Car 2 Go

Grab a car and go! They’re everywhere…seriously. Sometimes there’s two or more parked near our house. Downside is a $35 registration fee but after it’s $0.41/min with a $15 hour and $85 day max. One of the nice things though is you can park it and walk away, don’t have to pay for parking either. Although it may be gone by the time you go back to it and you’ll have to find another car (unles you don’t end the trip).

If you’re thinking about going this option setup an account before making the trip since there’s some setup in getting a members card and something something.

Trimet

Public transportion via trains & buses is pretty widespread going just about anywhere you could want to go. We’ve heard good things and bad things, mostly good though so that’s good. Can’t speak personally for Trimet as we’ve only taken the Max (train) from the airport once. On their website though there’s a handy trip planner give you an estimate of the time and route to get you where you want to go. There’s also an app (Android , iOS) where you can plan a trip, buy passes and see where the hell your bus is.
